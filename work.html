PlayStation

Telemetry for the whole platform
Sr. Data Product Manager, Telemetry & Streaming
Oct 2025 to Present

A platform the size of PlayStation runs on its data: the devices, the services, the ecosystem, and the games on top of it. When that data is messy or slow, good decisions die waiting for proof.

The bet

Treat telemetry like a product, not plumbing. Own the telemetry and streaming data roadmap across the platform, devices, and ecosystem, and make sure system behavior, player journeys, live features, and experiments are all measured the same trustworthy way.

What it delivers
  • Cleaner data and faster answers for teams across the company, not just game studios
  • Consistent measurement spanning platform, devices, services, and games
  • Data reliable enough to bet a roadmap on

ArenaNet

Six years inside Guild Wars 2
Fraud & Game Security Lead, then Analytics Lead & PM
May 2013 to Nov 2019

One of the most respected MMOs in the genre, lived in from two very different seats. First the job was keeping the world fair. Later it was keeping it thriving.

As Fraud & Game Security Lead (2013 to 2017)

Ran game security and economy integrity for live operations: spotting where new systems could be exploited, recommending fixes, and using player data to curb cheating and abuse without punishing honest players.

As Analytics Lead & Product Manager (2017 to 2019)

Owned the game's health metrics across expansions and seasons. Shipped live events between major releases, with rewards and pacing tuned from real behavior, plus the reporting to track how it all landed.

Why it mattered
  • Kept the in-game economy healthy and resistant to exploits as the game evolved
  • Shipped recurring in-game events that gave players reasons to return between major releases
  • Brought an attacker's eye for risk into every product and live-ops decision

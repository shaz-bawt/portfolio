📡 Telemetry game teams can trust

Sr. Data Product Manager, Telemetry & Streaming · PlayStation
Oct 2025 — Present

Game and product teams across PlayStation can only iterate as well as their data lets them. When instrumentation is inconsistent and insight is slow, good ideas die waiting for evidence.

The bet

Treat telemetry as a product, not plumbing. Own the roadmap for data quality, observability, and time-to-insight, and standardize how player journeys, live features, experimentation, and personalization are measured across the platform's surfaces.

What that looks like
  • A telemetry & streaming roadmap aimed squarely at studio decision-making speed
  • Consistent instrumentation standards so teams compare apples to apples
  • Requirements, success criteria, and stakeholder alignment that make measurement reliable enough to bet a roadmap on

⚔️ Six years inside Guild Wars 2

Analytics Lead & PM, then Fraud & Game Security Lead · ArenaNet
May 2013 — Nov 2019

One of the most respected MMOs in the genre, lived in from two very different seats. First the question was “how do we keep this world fair?” — later it became “how do we keep it thriving?”

As Fraud & Game Security Lead (2013–2017)

Led game security, exploit response, and economy integrity for live operations — spotting exploit risk in new systems, recommending design mitigations, and using behavior analysis to curb cheating, abuse, and toxicity while protecting the experience of legitimate players.

As Analytics Lead & Product Manager (2017–2019)

Owned business and gameplay KPI health across expansions and seasonal updates. Produced and shipped engagement events between major releases — reward structures, pacing, and goals tuned from real player behavior — and built the instrumentation and reporting for completion, economy balance, and retention.

Why it mattered
  • Protected the integrity of a beloved live MMO economy at scale
  • Shipped behavior-tuned live content that kept players engaged between releases
  • Carried hard-won security instincts straight into product decisions

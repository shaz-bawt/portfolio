▸ Character Bio

THE LORE

Part product manager, part analyst, part economy tuner — fully obsessed with why players do what they do.

▸ Origin Story

I've spent 15+ years inside games — AAA, MMO, live-service, and cross-platform — working at the seam where player behavior meets product strategy. I started close to the data (security, fraud, gameplay analytics) and grew into product leadership, but the throughline never changed: understand the player, then build the thing that serves them.

These days I focus on the systems that make live games feel alive — seasonal cadence, progression pacing, reward value, economy integrity, and the telemetry that tells you whether any of it is actually working. I'm a hypothesis-and-iteration person: form a bet, instrument it, read the signal, adjust.

I'm the partner who sits between design, engineering, production, and leadership and turns "players seem to be churning at the mid-game" into a tuning plan, a KPI framework, and a roadmap decision everyone can rally behind.

The story

From breaking games
to building teams.

I've spent almost twenty years in games, and I've come at them from nearly every angle: testing them in QA, designing them, defending their economies as a security lead, measuring them as an analyst, and leading the product and analytics work behind them. Each seat taught me something the next one needed. Today I do two things best: helping studios launch live-service games or mature the ones they have, and building and mentoring the analytics and product teams that keep them running.

Game security is a deeply technical craft, and what's really at stake is trust. Every exploit you close protects the players who play fair.

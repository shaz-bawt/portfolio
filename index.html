How I work

Three things I believe about live games

These are the ideas I keep proving out, studio after studio. They're the lens I bring to any team I join.

🔁

Live service is a craft

Launching a live game and maturing one are different jobs, and I've done both. Cadence, rewards, progression, economy, retention: I help a studio either find its live-service footing or sharpen the model it already runs.

🌱

Strong teams, better games

Behind every game that keeps players coming back is a team that can read them and respond. I build and mentor analytics and product groups and set up the rituals that keep them making the game better, release after release.

🎯

Evidence over assumptions

The strongest decisions come from what players actually do, paired with the instincts of the people who make the game. I turn open questions into clear bets, measure them, and give the team a shared read to act on.
